01 · What ODC is

ODC answers one boundary question

Under what conditions was this feature designed to operate?

Which roads, speeds, weather, and traffic environments are in scope?

In work zones, flooding, or weak lane markings, should the system suppress activation or exit?

Does that boundary come from a manual, an official source, or a third-party inference?

Capability and boundary transparency are different things. ODC is about the latter.

Boundary list

ODC turns real scenes into authorable conditions

The standard’s ODC elements cover road, infrastructure, objects, weather, digital information, personnel state, and vehicle state. “Supports highway driving” is not enough.

Road Infrastructure Objects Weather Digital info Personnel Vehicle state

Make ODC concrete: it is a structured set of conditions that can be authored, validated, and compared.

Do not confuse them

Automation level is not ODC

L2, L3, and L4 primarily describe responsibility and DDT allocation. ODC describes operating conditions. Two L2 systems can have very different boundaries across markets, models, and software versions.

L2Driver remains responsibleODC describes feature-use conditions, not permission to stop supervising.
L3System takes DDT within limitsODC must be tied to exit and takeover conditions.
L4Driverless within a domainGeofence, weather, and operating rules become core boundaries.

Prevent the “AD leaderboard” misunderstanding. OpenODC compares boundary clarity, not capability level.

02 · National-standard context

GB/T 45312-2025 gives a common language

The standard decomposes automated-driving operational design conditions into structured elements and requires permitted / not-permitted states, associations, and exit behavior.

7categories 144standard elements §5.4permitted / not permitted / association §5.5exit behavior

The standard matters because it gives OEMs, testing bodies, regulators, and researchers a shared object of discussion.

But

A standard is not a public platform

GB/T 45312-2025 provides the syntax, but it does not automatically produce an industry database. Manuals, official pages, operating rules, and tests are still scattered and hard to compare.

OEM wording differs

Evidence granularity differs

Third-party review is difficult

Consumers cannot see a unified boundary

This is the natural entry point for OpenODC: the industry needs an application layer between the standard and public evidence.

03 · Industry gap

What is missing is a public ODC data layer

That layer should connect standard elements, public-source extraction, provenance, version status, and comparison tools, so boundaries become queryable data instead of scattered manual prose.

GB/T 45312-2025
Public-source extraction
Evidence and confidence labels
Browse, compare, contribute

OpenODC enters as the public data layer from the standard to practical use.

OpenODC positioning

It measures how clearly public boundaries are evidenced

OpenODC is not a safety certification and not an AD leaderboard. Unless a record is marked vendor_confirmed, it is a community extraction from public sources.

No capability ranking

No replacement for OEM ODC systems

No replacement for ISO 21448 / ISO 26262 safety cases

No packaging inference as official disclosure

This slide protects the project from the most likely public misreadings.

04 · Method

Four method rules behind OpenODC

The reusable asset is not a nice page. It is a method for standard alignment, provenance, semantics, and versioned boundary data.

01

Standard-first

JSON Schema is the contract; pages are derivatives.

02

Provenance-first

30 sourced entries are better than 80 unsourced entries.

03

Semantics-first

L2/L3/L4 can share elements, but their meanings differ.

04

Combinations second

The element table is the base; combined scenes are trigger candidates.

This is the conceptual core of the talk.

Framework

One ODC document supports multiple views

Each sample is built around one ODCDocument: identity, elements[], associations[], boundary_combinations[], and metadata.

6public samples 144standard elements 60boundary combinations 5core views

Gallery, detail, Compare, Matrix, Editor, and Workbench are not separate ideas. They are views over the same data contract.

Why it is not just a table

Single-element permission does not mean combined-scene availability

An ODC table decomposes high-dimensional real scenes into single elements. Risk often appears when weather, lane markings, roads, objects, speed, and HMI stack together.

Rain / fog / glare
Worn lane markings
Work-zone rerouting
Driver distraction
This is not four independent cells. It is one combined-boundary problem.

Place boundary combinations after the methodology: first the table, then the limitation, then the second layer.

Boundary combinations

A second layer, not a safety conclusion

`boundary_combinations[]` records a small set of public-source-supported combinations: related elements, ODC relation, expected response, and evidence level.

01related_element_ids

Which ODC elements appear together.

02relation_to_odc

inside / outside / boundary / unknown.

03expected_response

Suppress activation, trigger exit, takeover, or not disclosed.

04evidence_level

Official, manual, operating rule, or public gap.

Stress that this second layer is deliberately lightweight. It structures public evidence without pretending to be a full SOTIF analysis.

Interface

From ODC to SOTIF trigger candidates

OpenODC does not build a safety case. It organizes public boundary evidence into traceable trigger-condition candidates so data and anomaly projects can continue the analysis.

OpenODCDeclared boundaries and combination candidates
DRIVEResearchReal-road exposure frequency and parameter distribution
ROAMBoundary exceedance, anomaly, and intervention outcome

OpenODC handles declared boundaries; DRIVEResearch handles exposure; ROAM handles outcomes.

Evidence governance

Evidence matters more than completeness

OpenODC does not try to fill every blank. Each item and combination must state whether it comes from a manual, official source, curation, inference, or a public-source gap.

Manual-backed Official statement Community curated Inferred Public-source gap Structural category

Coverage is not vendor disclosure rate. Gaps must remain visible.

05 · Summary

OpenODC in one sentence

OpenODC organizes GB/T 45312-2025 ODC elements, public-source evidence, and combined-boundary candidates into a verifiable, comparable, and co-maintained open data platform.

ODC names the boundary. The standard aligns the boundary. OpenODC makes the boundary publicly verifiable.

Do not introduce a new topic here. Use one sentence to close the concept-standard-platform arc.
