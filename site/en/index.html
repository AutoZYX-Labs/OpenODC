OpenODC

A public, machine-readable registry for AD operating boundaries.

OpenODC does not rank AD systems. It asks a narrower question: how much of a system's operational boundary is actually backed by public manuals, official pages, government rules, or clearly marked community extraction?

Public value

Turn “when can this feature be used, when should it exit, and what is not publicly stated” into a verifiable list, reducing the risk of confusing L2 assistance with automation.

Industry value

Use one 144-element national-standard vocabulary to compare public boundaries across OEMs, automation levels, and robotaxi operating domains.

Engineering value

Provide JSON Schema, an editor, compare views, a matrix view, and a vendor workbench so ODC moves from PDF prose into versioned, reviewable data.
